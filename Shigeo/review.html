Shigeo Kageyama

One of my favorite characters from an anime I really like.

Character

Shigeo Kageyama, nicknamed "Mob", is the main protagonist of the Mob Psycho 100 series. Mob is a Esper. Espers are humans that naturally awaken, possess and can harness psychic powers.

Powers and Abilities

  • Telekinesis
  • Spiritual Awareness
  • Astral Projection

Notes and trivia

  • Mob's favourite foods are takoyaki, milk and ramen.
  • His favourite music is the cheerful sounding ones and his favourite movie genre is action.
  • Apparently, Mob's speciality is making dogs float and his hobby is watching the sky.
